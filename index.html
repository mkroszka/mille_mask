MILLEDION is a strategic communications agency offering comprehensive, tailor-made PR & PA services for a private, public and non-governmental sector. Our strength derives from an attentive understanding of a client’s needs and assets, its market, public and political environment and its core competences vs imperfections. That's why we can prepare customized advantageous solutions, real-time response or platform of constructive dialogue (B2B, B2G, G2P).

As an independent, boutiqe agency MILLEDION cooperates with reliable and responsible professionals with 15 years of experience in the field of strategy-driven, creative & fruitful execution, in Poland and abroad. We act with a sense of responsibility and purpose, in the spirit of  getting things done properly, within a realistic attitude, timelines and metrics.

MILLEDION – will help you find and connect dots of your experiences and qualities, values and goals - to discover and tell the story soundly and effectively.
